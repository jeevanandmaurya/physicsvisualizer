Physics Showcase - Comprehensive Demonstration

This scene demonstrates all physics engine features:

PHYSICS OBJECTS:
- Bouncing sphere with high restitution
- Rotating cube with initial velocity
- Heavy cylinder with high mass
- Light capsule with low mass
- Cone with medium properties
- Ramp for rolling demonstrations

CONSTRAINTS:
- Spring pendulum system
- Chain system with multiple connected spheres
- Spring constraints with custom stiffness and damping

FLUID DYNAMICS:
- Fluid balls with drag and lift coefficients
- Simulated air resistance

VISUAL ANNOTATIONS:
- Velocity vectors on all moving objects
- Speed labels (dynamic text)
- Momentum displays
- Kinetic energy indicators
- Position coordinates
- Motion trails
- Multiple colors for different object types
